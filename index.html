🇧🇩 Bangladesh Job Market Visualizer

Interactive treemap of 46 occupations covering ~66M workers across Bangladesh's economy. Area = headcount. Color = selected metric. Data: BBS Labour Force Survey 2023 · BGMEA · BMET · BBS Wage Survey. Inspired by Andrej Karpathy's US Job Market Visualizer.

Sources: BBS LFS 2023 · BGMEA · BMET · Bangladesh Bank · Glassdoor BD · PayScale BD · BBS National Pay Scale 2015 Blueprint: Bangladesh Jobs Visualizer v1.0 · Architecture adapted from karpathy/jobs
